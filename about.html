About Us

Canadian Horse Properties is Canadas Exclusive Equestrian Real Estate Marketplace.

CanadianHorseProperties.com is a new, bold, easy to navigate, horse property specific real estate website. Whether you are an individual just dabbling with the idea of buying a horse farm or an experienced real estate agent listing multiple equestrian properties for sale, this is your targeted online horse property resource.

We make buying, selling, or searching for your Canadian dream horse property easier. Enter your horse property search type, your results will include horse specific property information which you can navigate and compare with other listings. Then connect directly to the listing agents or owners for further information.

CanadianHorseProperties.com gives both the buyer and seller a focused place to search for or list a horse property for sale or rent in Canada. We understand horse farms. We are Canadian horse property owners, helping fellow Canadians buy or sell their perfect horse property.

Canadian Horse Properties qualifies properties of 3.0 Acres or more as Horse Properties. Typical Canadian
rural bylaws require a minimum of 3.0 acres to accommodate a horse.
